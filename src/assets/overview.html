Welcome

Welcome to Centre Stage Online, a part of the Centre Stage project, a European collaborative project for professional women artists in the performing arts and music. It’s a project that aims to contribute to positive change at both individual and structural levels for women performing artists in Europe. We hope that you will benefit from this course and please let us know what you think in the evaluation at the end.

Katarina Hultin, Project manager

Some practicalities before beginning the course...


  • Get a nice and inspiring notebook before you get started, which you'll use throughout the course
  • Take the course units in the correct order (1–14)
  • You can best assimilate the course content by taking the course via computer or tablet
  • We recommend that you use the same device repeatedly so that the course page remembers you as a user
  • Once you have registered, you will remain logged in on your default language if you use the same device repeatedly
  • During the course you will be instructed to fill in worksheets. You can choose to download all at once or gradually, during each course unit
  • You take the course at your own pace
