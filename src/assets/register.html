Registration

To take the course you need to register on this page. The course is free of charge and anyone who is an artistic professional in the performing arts and music is welcome to take the course. You take the course completely at your own pace. By registering, you do not commit to anything.

We recommend that you use a computer or tablet when you take the course (this is because you will download worksheets). We also recommend that you then use the same device repeatedly so that the course page recognizes you as a user. Once you have registered, you will remain logged in, in your default language.

We protect your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and its principles that personal data should be processed in a lawful, accurate and transparent manner in relation to you as a registered participant. Read more about how your personal data is protected here.

The reason why registration is necessary to take the course is that we want to prevent the dissemination of the course content and that we need the collection of statistics for the project's goal tracking.
